Our Healthcare Services

🏥

Specialist Referrals

Connecting families with pediatric specialists, surgeons, and medical experts for specialized care.

🩺

Primary Healthcare

Access to regular check-ups, vaccinations, and preventive care for children with congenital conditions.

💊

Medication Support

Providing essential medications, treatments, and medical supplies at no cost to families in need.

🔬

Diagnostic Testing

Access to laboratory tests, imaging, and genetic screening for early and accurate diagnosis.

❤️

Surgical Interventions

Life-changing surgeries for children with congenital heart defects, cleft conditions, and other needs.

📋

Care Coordination

Comprehensive case management ensuring continuity of care across multiple providers.

🏥 How Our Referral System Works

1. Assessment

Initial evaluation of child's medical needs

2. Referral

Connection to appropriate specialist or hospital

3. Coordination

Appointment scheduling and logistics support

4. Follow-up

Post-treatment monitoring and ongoing care
