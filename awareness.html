🌟 Why Awareness Matters

Stigma and misinformation about congenital conditions prevent many children from accessing the care and support they need. Our awareness programs work to:

✅ Reduce Stigma

Challenging myths and misconceptions

✅ Promote Early Detection

Encouraging timely medical care

✅ Foster Inclusion

Building welcoming communities

✅ Empower Families

Connecting families with resources
