Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, Nawe Abeho Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
How much of my donation goes to programs?
90% of every donation goes directly to our programs and services for children and families.
Can I make a recurring donation?
Yes, you can select monthly or yearly donation options to provide sustained support.
Will I receive a receipt?
Yes, you will receive an email receipt immediately after your donation.
