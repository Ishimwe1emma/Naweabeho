Conditions We Raise Awareness About

❤️

Congenital Heart Defects

Structural heart problems present at birth that affect how blood flows through the heart.

🦷

Cleft Lip & Palate

Opening or split in the upper lip, roof of the mouth, or both that occurs during early pregnancy.

🦴

Clubfoot

Condition where a baby's foot is twisted out of shape or position at birth.

🧠

Neural Tube Defects

Birth defects of the brain, spine, or spinal cord that happen in the first month of pregnancy.

🩸

Sickle Cell Disease

Genetic blood disorder that causes red blood cells to become misshapen and break down.

👁️

Vision & Hearing Loss

Sensory impairments that can be present at birth or develop in early childhood.

🩺 Early Detection Saves Lives

Many congenital conditions can be effectively treated if detected early. Early diagnosis allows for timely intervention, better outcomes, and improved quality of life for affected children.
